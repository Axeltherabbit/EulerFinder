What is cpu contribute?

Cpu contribute is your contribute for the site maintenance and updates,
you are free to disable it when you want in the EulerFinder mainpage through the list beside the "feedback" button

Is it dangerous for my CPU?

No it's not dangerous because by default script limits the cpu usage at 50%,
but you can rise or decrease this limit when you want through the list beside the "feedback" button in the main page.
